**1. validation-results.md — Aggregated analysis**

Show CONFIRMS count vs DOES NOT CONFIRM count.

Analysis sections:
- **Pain patterns:** What bottleneck(s) were named most frequently? Were they the same ones identified in research, or different?
- **Cost quantification:** What numbers did decision-makers mention? (hours/week, revenue lost, complaints/month)
- **Prior attempts:** What solutions have they tried? Why did they fail? (This feeds Phase 3 pitch)
- **Unexpected insights:** Anything that changes the thesis — new pain points, different buyer profile than expected, pricing signals
- **Strongest quotes:** Top 3-5 verbatim quotes that could be used in sales materials (with permission)
- **Verdict:** [CONFIRMS count] / 5 conversations confirmed the pain. Threshold: 3/5.

**2. niche-declaration.md — Final niche selection document**

3. Customer journey: [top 3 friction points] (reference research-niche-X.md)
4. Gap analysis: [2+ gaps identified] (reference research-niche-X.md)
5. Demand validation: [CONFIRMS count]/5 decision-makers confirmed pain (reference validation-results.md)

- [pain 1 with quote]
- [pain 2 with quote]
- [pain 3 with quote]

IMPLICATIONS FOR NEXT PHASES:
- Phase 2 (Revenue Model): [pricing signals from conversations]
- Phase 3 (Sales System): [pitch ammunition from pain quotes]

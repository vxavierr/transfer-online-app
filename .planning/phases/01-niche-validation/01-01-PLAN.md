<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 2: Score all candidates through Go/No-go gate</name>
  <files>
    .planning/phases/01-niche-validation/outputs/gate-results.md
  </files>
  <action>
For each of the 6-8 candidates from candidate-longlist.md, fill out a complete gate scoring sheet using the template from Task 1.

**Research approach per candidate (use web search via EXA MCP if available, otherwise use knowledge + WebFetch for IBGE/Google):**

For each criterion, gather evidence:
1. **Payment capacity** — Search what software/tools this niche typically pays for in Brazil. Look for SaaS products targeting this sector, monthly price ranges. Cross-reference with SEBRAE sector data if findable.
2. **Visible operational bottleneck** — Search Google/LinkedIn for complaints, forum posts, or articles about operational pain in this niche. Look for keywords like "demora", "perda de cliente", "atendimento ruim", "processo manual".
3. **Repetitive processes** — List at least 3 daily/weekly repetitive processes. Use knowledge of the sector or search for "rotina [niche]" / "processos [niche]".
4. **WhatsApp as primary channel** — Search "whatsapp [niche] atendimento brasil". Check if the sector commonly uses WhatsApp Business for client communication.
5. **Accessible decision-makers** — Assess whether the business owner decides alone (small firms) vs committee (large firms). Consider typical firm size in this niche.

**Gate scoring rules:**
- Each criterion: PASS or FAIL with evidence cited
- Any single FAIL = ELIMINATE (no exceptions)
- Document reasoning even for eliminated candidates (helps explain the decision later)

**Output structure in gate-results.md:**
- Header with date and evaluator
- One complete gate scoring sheet per candidate (use the template)
- Summary table at bottom: Niche | C1 | C2 | C3 | C4 | C5 | Result
- Final section: "SURVIVORS FOR DEEP RESEARCH" listing the 3 niches that passed all 5 criteria

**If more than 3 pass:** Keep all that pass but note which 3 are recommended for the user's shortlist (per user decision: "Shortlist of exactly 3 niches to compare"). Rank by strength of evidence.

**If fewer than 3 pass:** Flag this clearly. Note which candidates came closest (fewest FAILs) and recommend either: (a) relaxing a criterion with justification, or (b) generating additional candidates to gate.

**Anti-pattern guard:** Do NOT research with confirmation bias. For each candidate, actively look for reasons it should FAIL before looking for reasons it should PASS.
  </action>
